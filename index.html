THE ACRE DEAL - LEAD SOURCING FRAMEWORK

PURPOSE

This framework provides Land Intake Managers (LIMs) with a systematic approach to source qualified land seller leads through effective phone conversations.

GOAL

To ensure every lead meets THREE critical criteria before being "sourced" and presented with an offer:

THE THREE SOURCING CRITERIA

1. IF - Interest Factor ‚úÖ

  • Seller expresses clear interest in selling the land
  • Not just curious but genuinely considering a sale
  • Ready to move forward with the right offer

2. PRICE FLEXIBILITY üí∞

  • Seller understands investor pricing vs retail pricing
  • Willing to entertain offers below full market value
  • Values speed and convenience over maximum price

3. WHY - Motivation üéØ

  • Seller has a clear reason for selling now
  • Could be financial need, life change, or property burden
  • The "why" drives their decision timeline

YOUR ROLE AS A LIM

Your job is to be articulate and empathetic on the phone to understand the seller's situation and determine which side of the fence they're on:

üü¢ GREEN LIGHT SELLERS:

  • Interested in selling
  • Have a need for money or want to offload burden
  • Accept investor pricing for speed and convenience

üî¥ RED LIGHT SELLERS:

  • Not genuinely interested
  • No obvious need to sell
  • Firm on retail price

USING THIS SCRIPT

The following 8-phase script is designed to naturally uncover these three criteria through conversational flow:

  • Phase 1-2: Establish Interest (IF)
  • Phase 3: Uncover Motivation (WHY)
  • Phase 4: Set Price Expectations (PRICE FLEXIBILITY)
  • Phase 5-8: Verify and Close

2

INTEREST CHECK (IF)

Determine if selling is on their radar

~30 sec
AGENT: "Here's why I'm calling: We're a boutique family-owned land investment operation that specializes in [State/County]. We've closed over 1,000 deals. Unlike listing with an agent, we make cash offers, close in the next few weeks, and handle all the paperwork. No fees, no commissions, no waiting around.

The reason I'm reaching out specifically to you is we're actively buying in your area.

Real quick - is selling this land even on your radar, or I might be wasting both our time here?"

Alternative Questions:

  • "Quick question - would selling this property solve any problems for you, or is it not even on your mind?"
  • "Before I take up more of your time - is selling something you're actually considering, or am I way off here?"
SELLER: "Actually, I have been thinking about selling" / "Yeah, I might be interested" / "I'd consider it for the right price" Continue to Phase 3
SELLER: "No, I'm not looking to sell" / "Not interested in selling" / "We're keeping the land"
AGENT: "I understand. Is that a 'not interested' for now, or is that a 'not interested' forever?" NURTURE/DNC CHECK
3

MOTIVATION (WHY) + TIMELINE (WHEN)

Uncover their reason for selling and timeline

~2 min
AGENT: "Excellent. Let me ask you this - did you buy that land or inherit it?"
PATH A - SELLER: "I inherited it" / "It was my father's" / "Got it when my parents passed"
AGENT: "I see. How long ago did you inherit it?"
SELLER: "About [X] years ago"
AGENT: "A lot of folks who inherit land tell me it becomes more of a burden than a blessing. Has that been your experience?"
PATH B - SELLER: "I bought it" / "We purchased it as an investment"
AGENT: "Got it. How long ago did you buy it?"
SELLER: "[X] years ago"
AGENT: "What was your original idea when you bought it - were you going to use it for something specific?"

4

PRICE EXPECTATIONS

Set investor pricing expectations

~1 min

Choose Your Approach:

Version 1 - Mutual Benefit Focus:
"Before we go any further, I want to make sure we're a right fit for you and our investment needs. We take all the risk here - buying sight unseen, as-is, no inspections, no contingencies. That level of risk means our offer includes a safety margin. But here's the thing - we typically pay 20-50% more than other investors because we actually understand land value and have the funds ready. Sure, you might squeeze out more dollars with a realtor over 6-12 months, minus their hefty commission. But we're offering something different - guaranteed cash in weeks with zero hassle on your end. Does that type of arrangement work for what you're trying to accomplish?"
Version 2 - Partnership Tone:
"Let me share how we work to see if we're a right fit for you and our investment needs. We take all the risk here - buying sight unseen, as-is, no inspections, no contingencies. That level of risk means our offer includes a safety margin. However, we're not bottom-feeders - we typically offer 20-50% more than other land investors out there. Sure, you might squeeze out more dollars with a realtor over 6-12 months, minus their hefty commission. But we're offering something different - guaranteed cash in weeks with zero hassle on your end. Would this approach align with your goals?"
Version 3 - Direct and Clear:
"I want to be transparent to ensure we're a right fit for you and our investment needs. We take all the risk here - buying sight unseen, as-is, no inspections, no contingencies. That level of risk means our offer includes a safety margin. Now, we're the highest-paying land investors you'll likely talk to - typically 20-50% more than the competition. Sure, you might squeeze out more dollars with a realtor over 6-12 months, minus their hefty commission. But we're offering something different - guaranteed cash in weeks with zero hassle on your end. Does that sound like something that would work for your situation?"
SELLER: "Yes, that works for me" / "Speed is more important" / "I just want it gone"
AGENT: "Perfect! Sounds like we're on the same page." Continue to Phase 5
SELLER: "I need top dollar" / "I want full market value"
AGENT: "I totally understand. Just remember - realtors charge 10% commission and average 6-12 months, and about 30% of deals fall through. Would you like our offer anyway to compare against other options?" EDUCATE & OFFER
5

OWNERSHIP VERIFICATION

Confirm decision makers

~30 sec
AGENT: "Just to confirm - are you the sole owner, or are there other people on the title?"
SELLER: "It's just me" / "I'm the only owner"
AGENT: "Perfect, that makes things simple." Continue
SELLER: "My spouse and I own it" / "It's me and my siblings" / "There are multiple owners"
AGENT: "Got it. Are all the owners on board with selling, or do you need to have some conversations first?"
SELLER: "It's part of an estate" / "We inherited it but haven't transferred the title yet"
AGENT: "Do you have 100% authority to sell, or is the title work still being sorted out?"
Note: Our underwriting team will review on a case-by-case basis.
6

ACCESS VERIFICATION

Check property access

~30 sec
AGENT: "Does the property have road frontage, or do you access it through an easement?"
SELLER: "It's right on the road" / "Has street frontage"
AGENT: "Perfect, that's ideal." Continue
SELLER: "There's an easement" / "You go through another property"
AGENT: "Is that a recorded easement? Legal and permanent?"
SELLER: "I'm not really sure" / "I don't know"
AGENT: "No worries. Can you physically drive to the property?"
Key Phrase for Unclear Situations:
"Look, I'm not the expert on title and access issues - that's what our underwriting team does. Let me get all your information, and they'll dig into the details to see what's possible. We deal with complicated situations all the time."
7

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

Quick details to complete the file

~30 sec
AGENT: "Just a few more quick details:"

Questions to Ask:

  • "How long have you owned the property?"
  • "Have you made any improvements to the property?"
  • "What's the best way to send you our offer - email or regular mail?"
  • "Do you own any other properties you might consider selling?"
SELLER: "Email is fine" / "Send it to my email"
AGENT: "Perfect. Can you confirm your email address for me?"
SELLER: "Actually, I have a few other properties"
AGENT: "Oh great! Are they also in [State/County], or elsewhere?"

Wrap up and set expectations

~30 sec
AGENT - QUICK SUMMARY:
"Great! So just to confirm - you own [acreage] in [County], you're interested in selling, and you'd like to receive our cash offer by [email/mail]. Did I get everything?"
SELLER: "Yes, that's right" / "Sounds good"
AGENT - NEXT STEPS:
"Perfect. Here's what happens next:

I'll get this to our underwriting team on a priority basis. You'll receive our cash offer within the next few hours. Let me just confirm - I'm sending this to [email/phone] - is that correct?"
AGENT - FINAL CLOSE:
"Excellent. Is there anything else I can answer for you before we wrap up?"

SELLER: "No, I think that's it"

AGENT: "Perfect! Look for that offer in the next few hours. Thanks for your time, [Name]!"
IF SELLER ASKS: "How much are you offering?" / "What's the price?"
AGENT: "I need our team to run the numbers to give you our best offer. You'll have it in the next few hours. Fair?"

Lead Scoring Quick Reference:

  • HOT (8-10): Definitely selling + price flexible + urgent timeline
  • WARM (6-7): Probably selling + some flexibility
  • COLD (4-5): Just curious + wants retail
  • NURTURE: Not now, maybe later
  • DNC: Never interested
