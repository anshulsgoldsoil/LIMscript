Lead Scoring Quick Reference:

  • DISTRESS (10): Urgent! Crisis situation
  • HOT (8-9): Definitely selling + price flexible
  • WARM (7): Probably selling + some flexibility
  • LWARM (6): Maybe interested + needs convincing
  • COLD (5): Just curious + wants retail
  • NURTURE (4): Not now, maybe later
  • DD (3): Ownership/Access issues
  • DNC (2): Never! Don't call!
  • WRNG (1): Wrong number
  • UNDETER (0): Can't determine ownership or interest
